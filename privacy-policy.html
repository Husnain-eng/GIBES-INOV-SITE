GIBES INOV Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 6, 2024

1. Introduction & Compliance Framework

This policy outlines how GIBES INOV collects, uses, and protects personal data. We are committed to high data protection standards, including compliance with the **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)** for data subjects in the European Economic Area (EEA) and the applicable data privacy laws in **Pakistan**.

2. Data We Collect & Lawful Basis (GDPR)

Lead Capture Forms (Audit/Contact)

Data: Name, Company, Email, Phone, Region, Problem Description, Budget.

Lawful Basis: **Consent** (explicit opt-in checkbox) and **Legitimate Interest** (to fulfill a pre-contractual request for a quote/audit).

Usage Data (Analytics)

Data: IP address (anonymized), browser type, pages viewed, time on site, clicks.

Lawful Basis: **Consent** via our Cookie Consent Management Platform (CMP). Data is anonymized for European users via Google Analytics 4 settings (IP anonymization, regional data restrictions).

3. Data Use and Sharing

We use your data exclusively to:

  • Respond to your inquiry and provide the free diagnostic audit.
  • Process the project request and prepare a scope-of-work (SOW) agreement.
  • Improve our website performance and marketing efforts (only with consent).
  • Share data with necessary third-party processors (CRM, email service) who are also contractually obligated to maintain GDPR/PK standards.

4. Your Rights (GDPR & Pakistan)

You have the right to access, rectify, or erase your personal data. For our European users, these are defined by GDPR (e.g., Right to Erasure, Data Portability). For our Pakistan users, we apply these best practices as a standard for transparent operations. To exercise these rights, please email us at privacy@gibesinov.com.

5. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored on secure, encrypted servers. Data belonging to European subjects is processed using server-side tracking and stored in EU-based cloud infrastructure where feasible. All client data is encrypted both in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest.
